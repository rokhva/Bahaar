The Labs Environment

The new Labs environment allows for gathering customer feedback on a larger scale. And on a much wider range of potential solutions.

Problem

Customer feedback guides and shapes the final output during every step of the research process (discovery, concept validation, design, usability and testing). However, the team is unable to interview all, or even the majority of the customer base.

Approach

How can we involve our customers earlier in the process? It would be ideal to get feedback before features and improvements were released into the product. Our research process into how to scale research (a bit of a research tur-ducken I know) began with who we identified as our early adopters and visionaries. Users who were active on Adobe Analytics forums, and open community slack channels. We wanted to know how they wanted to shape the product they spent so much time in day to day.

Solution

A dedicated place within Adobe Analytics where users can test out in progress, designs, user interface improvements, and algorithms. A true win-win set up. Users get to see what the future could look like, and weigh in on what they really need. And the product teams get a more complete understanding of what the user base needs and wants.
